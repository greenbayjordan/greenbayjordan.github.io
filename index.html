About

Jordan Packer is a senior sociology major at UC San Diego, minoring in Urban Studies and Planning. Her primary interests are sociology of education, gender, and social networks. She often likes to explore the cross-section of these disciplines with ethnographic work, writing about the patterns of human behavior that create stratification. Jordan is a research assistant for the Center for Research on Gender in STEMM, where she analyzes the tangible differences men and women face when pursuing tenure-track faculty positions. She plans to pursue a PhD in Sociology herself after graduation, with the hope of becoming a professor down the road.

About me.
